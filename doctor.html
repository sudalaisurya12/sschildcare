Know Your Doctor

Dr. Dhanasekhar Kesavelu

Dr. Dhanasekhar Kesavelu our chief at SS Child Care has more than 18 years of experience in treating children with simple or complex medical problems is a Consultant Paediatric Gastroenterologist at Apollo Children hospital Chennai and an Honorary tutor at School of Developmental and Reproductive medicine at University of Liverpool, UK. He has publications in his name at various national and international journals and his constant endeavour of research on various subjects of paediatric gut health is aimed at making the lives of Children better.

A Note from the doctor about his vision

I would like to see myself as a consultant actively involved in teaching. I strongly believe in passing the skills I have acquired in my career to young and aspiring paediatricians and to those considering a career in the same. I find Paediatric Gastroenterology fascinating as it has a good balance of clinical and ‘hands on’ paediatrics, I have achieved these skills by training in one of the reputable European centres in paediatric gastroenterology, Alder hey children’s hospital, Liverpool and Sheffield Children’s hospital.
